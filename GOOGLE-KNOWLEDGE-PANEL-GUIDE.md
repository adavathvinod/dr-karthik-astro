## üÜò Common Issues & Solutions

### "My rich results aren't showing"
- **Wait**: Can take 1-3 weeks after deployment
- **Test**: Use Rich Results Test tool
- **Fix**: Any schema errors found
- **Request**: Submit to Search Console

### "No Knowledge Panel yet"
- **Normal**: Takes 3-6 months minimum
- **Focus**: Build citations and reviews first
- **Alternative**: Local knowledge box is success too
- **Patience**: Google needs to verify entity authority

### "Low Google ranking"
- **Content**: Add more pages (services, blog, FAQs)
- **Backlinks**: Get links from health directories
- **Reviews**: Collect more patient testimonials
- **Local SEO**: Optimize for "near me" searches
